Sloppy Joes

Description

A Sloppy Joe is a sandwich made up of mince beef and onions in a tomato-based sauce, usually served on a hamburger bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean mince beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup ketchup, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Cook the beef, onion, and green pepper.
  2. Drain the liquids, then stir.
  3. Simmer for about half an hour.
  4. Serve on hamburger buns.
